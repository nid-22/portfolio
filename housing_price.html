Exploring Data-Driven Investment Strategies in the Australian Real Estate Market!

Project Overview

Surprise Housing, a US-based real estate company, is venturing into the Australian market. The company is leveraging data analytics to make strategic property investments and maximize returns.

Data Source

The analysis uses a data set collected from Australian house sales (CSV file provided).

Objectives

  1. Build a regression model with regularization to predict the actual value of prospective properties.
  2. Identify significant variables influencing house prices.
  3. Determine how well these variables describe house prices.
  4. Find optimal values of lambda for ridge and lasso regression.

Business Goal

The goal is to model house prices using independent variables, providing actionable insights for strategic decision-making. Management aims to:

  • Understand price dynamics in the Australian market,
  • Focus on high-return areas, and
  • Optimize investment strategies for successful market entry.
