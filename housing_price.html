Exploring Data-Driven Investment Strategies in the Australian Real Estate Market!

Project Overview:

Surprise Housing, a US-based real estate company, ventures into the Australian market using data analytics to make strategic property investments.

Data Source:

Data set collected from Australian house sales (CSV file provided).

Objectives:

  1. Build a regression model with regularization to predict the actual value of prospective properties.
  2. Identify significant variables influencing house prices.
  3. Determine how well these variables describe house prices.
  4. Find optimal values of lambda for ridge and lasso regression.

Business Goal:

Model house prices with independent variables, providing insights for strategic decision-making. Management aims to understand price dynamics, focus on high-return areas, and optimize investment strategies in the new market.
