Key Features

1. Sign Up for Organisation

2. Sign Up for Individual

3. Landing Page

The landing page displays scheme cards, each representing a specific government initiative. The cards are differentiated based on the types of schemes, making it easy for users to identify and explore relevant opportunities.

4. Landing Page After Login

5. Scheme Page

After logging in, users are greeted with a dynamic sidebar that allows them to filter government schemes based on various criteria such as industry, sector, gender, and age group. This provides a highly personalized experience and ensures users can easily find schemes relevant to them.
